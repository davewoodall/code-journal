"The Scroll of Yochanan" or "The book of John"

Chapter 1

In the beginning the Son was Eloah.

Reference: John 1:1:1
Keywords
Eloah
Singular of 'Elohim'. It is improperly translated as 'God'. In Hebrew the term 'luck' or 'fortune' when spoken sounds like 'god'. There is no reason to be casual about mistranslations, especially when there are many people who pray to fortune tellers, believe in superstitions, and choose any pagan practice above worshiping the Most High.
